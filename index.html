Thinking of Building Something Awesome.

The Build Actual Stuff Hackathon is a 12-hour Hackathon geared at getting students to learn more by doing and shifting the paradigm of traditional hackathons away from being a competitive driven space to being one where individuals irrespective of their educational background can come together learn and hack solutions in a welcoming and condusive environment.
Africa is gradually becoming a frontier for Innovation. Over the last couple of years, we have witnessed a high growth in the use of technology and rapid pace of adoption. This in turn has lead to an increase in need for individuals with the knack for problem solving. We believe We can build the Africa We want.

What is a Hackathon

A hackathon is popularly known as an “invention marathon”. It offers everyone who has an interest in technology a welcoming and condusive atmosphere to learn, build & share their projects within 12-48hrs. You don’t have to be a Computer Science major or a programmer to attend.

Taking things up a notch

Get accesss to a suite of tools, APIs and resources when you attend a Build Actual Stuff Hackathon. Also you have the opportunity attend CodeLabs hosted by industrial experts on technologies you love.
